Graphics Card Market

1. NVIDIA Graphics Cards

GeForce Series (Consumer/Gaming):

GeForce RTX 4090: High-end card with exceptional performance, suitable for 4K gaming, real-time ray tracing, and AI-enhanced applications.
GeForce RTX 4080: Slightly lower performance than the 4090 but still excellent for high-resolution gaming and ray tracing.
GeForce RTX 4070: Mid-range option offering good performance for 1440p gaming with ray tracing support.
GeForce RTX 4060: More budget-friendly option for 1080p gaming, still capable of ray tracing and AI features like DLSS.
GeForce GTX 1660 Super: Older generation card, still popular for 1080p gaming without ray tracing.

2. AMD Graphics Cards

Radeon Series (Consumer/Gaming):

Radeon RX 7900 XTX: High-end card for 4K gaming, competing directly with NVIDIA's top-tier GPUs.
Radeon RX 6900 XT: Previous generation's high-end card, still very capable for high-resolution gaming.
Radeon RX 6800 XT: Mid-to-high range card offering excellent performance for 1440p and 4K gaming.
Radeon RX 6700 XT: Mid-range option, good for 1440p gaming with decent ray tracing capabilities.
Radeon RX 6600: Budget-friendly card for 1080p gaming, more energy-efficient.
